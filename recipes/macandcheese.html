Mac and Cheese

Description

Skip the boxed stuff and make homemade mac and cheese tonight. This from-scratch macaroni and cheese recipe will become a staple in your dinner rotation.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz uncooked elbow macaroni: This homemade mac and cheese starts with a box of uncooked macaroni noodles.
  • 1/4 cup salted butter and 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour: You'll need butter and flour to make a roux for the cheese sauce. You'll also need two tablespoons of butter for the topping.
  • 2 1/2 cups of milk: Use whole milk for the richest flavor and texture.
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese and 1/2 cups finely grated Parmesan cheese: This baked mac and cheese recipe calls for Cheddar and Parmesan cheeses.
  • Salt and Ground Pepper to taste: Salt and pepper goes into the sauce, while paprika is sprinkled over the topping.
  • 2 tablespoons salted butter, 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs and 1 pinch ground paprika: Use store-bought dried bread crumbs or make your own at home.
