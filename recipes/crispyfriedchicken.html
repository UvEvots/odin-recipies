Crispy Fried Chicken

Description

This is the only crispy fried chicken recipe you'll ever need. Why? It combines all the tried-and-true tricks to ensure the juiciest, most flavorful, crispiest chicken every time.

Ingredients

Yields 8 servings.
  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces: Cut a four-pound whole chicken into pieces or purchase four pounds of breasts, drumsticks, wings, legs, and/or thighs at the grocery store.
  • 1 cup buttermilk: Acidic buttermilk tenderizes the chicken without making it too tough. Also, it helps the flour mixture stick to the chicken.
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour for coating: All-purpose flour gives the buttermilk and seasonings something to stick to, while ensuring a wonderfully crispy crust.
  • 1 teaspoon paprika & salt and pepper to taste: This crispy fried chicken recipe calls for paprika (which helps with browning), salt, and pepper. You can add more spices and seasonings to taste.
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying: Vegetable oil is perfect for frying chicken because it has a high smoke point.
